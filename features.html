Researchers are people too

Most research software turns out to be ugly and hard to use. Not ours. We recognise that researchers shouldn't have to battle their software to get results. We understand that researchers are people too, and so we approach software design from the user's point of view, making sure we test our assumptions with real users along the way.

Standards matter

We believe in building for the future and supporting the past. First released in 2005, Geneious is one of the world’s leading bioinformatics software platforms, used by over 2,500 universities and institutes and commercial companies in more than 65 countries.

Geneious is used by all 20 of the top 20 Universities globally (Times Higher Education, 2012) and by seven of the ten largest Pharmaceutical companies.

With Geneious you can:

  • Identify novel or pathogenic mutational variants with powerful re-sequencing workflows,
  • Identify novel strains or isolates with de novo sequencing & assembly,
  • Identify large genomic events with multi-genome comparisons
  • Analyze any NGS data (Illumina, 454 Ion Torrent, Ion Proton, or PacBio),
  • Combine NGS and Sanger data for hybrid data analyzes.
  • Perform diversity studies with 16S or MLST and
  • Annotate whole bacterial genomes using Glimmer.
